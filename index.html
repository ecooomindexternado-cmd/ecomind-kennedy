Live data is only a snapshot

PM2.5 changes with wind, rain, traffic intensity and hour of the day. A low current value does not erase long-term exposure.

Our poster explains the structural reason for focusing on Kennedy: freight corridors, industrial activity and historical exposure.

Historical OAB analysis reports that Kennedy and Carvajal-Sevillana have been among the stations with the highest annual PM2.5 averages in Bogotá.

How PM2.5 affects people

Fine particles from mobile sources can enter through breathing and affect health.
